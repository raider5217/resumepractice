About Me

My name is Jason. I'm on my way to 50 years old and I finally figured out what I'd like to do in life. I wish I'd have found out sooner, but better late than never. I have a beautiful Wife and three great kids. Pain in the rear sometimes, but who isn't, right? My job...my job is ok. I don't hate my job. But it's just that...a job. I don't really get any fulfillment from it. Pays the bills. I found HTML, CSS around 10 years ago (give or take). Didn't really do anything outside of messing around every now again. Didn't put ANY effort into it. Really only looked at it a few times over the ten years. Job came first, "concentrate on the job. Make the money. work, home, sleep. repeat". Then about a year ago I felt the pull again. But, this time I didn't push it down. Still busy with work and learning stuff there, but I'm playing around a lot more. Building little things here and there. Not the greatest but fun for me. I figure if I spend hours upon hours trying to find mistakes I've made in my code and am not getting angry, then I really like it. I have given myself a goal of about two years to mostly understand HTML, CSS & JS. (I know others are laughing at the prospect of it taking me a couple of years but, so what. The more we work on something the better it becomes.). I hope that anyone who reads this will leave a constructive commet. Thank for your time.
